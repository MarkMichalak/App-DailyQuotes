1. Introduction

DailyQuotes (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store data when you use our mobile application DailyQuotes.

2. Information We Collect

  • Local Data: We store your favorite quotes locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share a quote.
  • Remote API: We fetch quotes from third-party APIs (e.g., quotable.io). We do not log or store any personally identifiable information from these calls.
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or any tracking identifiers.

3. Use of Data

Your favorites are saved so you can view them offline. No data is used for marketing or shared with advertisers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Quotable API under its terms of service. Please review their policy for details on how they handle requests.

5. Security

We follow industry best practices to secure local data on your device. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

DailyQuotes is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date will reflect any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at mark.michalak@hotmail.com.
