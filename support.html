Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is my quote not refreshing daily?
    DailyQuotes fetches a new quote once every 24 hours. If you reinstall the app or clear data, it will reset the cycle.
  • Can I browse older or themed quotes?
    Swipe left/right to browse previous or next days. Category filters (Stoic, Motivational, etc.) are coming soon!
  • How do I save my favorites?
    Tap the ❤️ icon under any quote to add it to your Favorites tab. You can remove or share them from there.
  • Is there an offline mode?
    Yes—your daily quote and all your Favorites are stored locally, so you can view them even without Internet.
