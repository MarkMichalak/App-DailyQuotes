Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is my quote not refreshing daily?
    The app fetches a new quote once every 24 hours. Clearing app data or reinstall will reset the cycle.
  • How do I save my favorites?
    Tap the ❤️ icon to add a quote to your Favorites. Manage them anytime from the Favorites tab.
  • Can I browse older or themed quotes?
    Swipe left/right to see yesterday’s quotes. Themed categories are coming soon!
  • Is there an offline mode?
    Yes—your daily quote and favorites are cached locally so you can read them without Internet.
